OHSS DATA SUPPRESSION — Documented 2026-05-24

The DHS Office of Homeland Security Statistics (OHSS) monthly
immigration enforcement tables were last published on January 16, 2025,
covering data through November 2024.

No new monthly tables have been published for any month in 2025 or 2026.
The Trump administration has stopped releasing this statistical series.
